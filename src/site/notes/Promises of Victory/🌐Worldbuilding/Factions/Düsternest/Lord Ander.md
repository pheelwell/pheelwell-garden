# Appearance
Lord Ander is a middle-aged human male with a strong build, hardened by his years as a war veteran. He bears an imposing scar across his face, which only serves to emphasize his air of authority. Despite his fierce appearance, he exudes warmth and approachability. As the ruler of [[Promises of Victory/üåêWorldbuilding/Regions/üè∞Duesternest/Duesternest\|Duesternest]], he is dedicated to promoting peace, prosperity, and the arts within his city. His appreciation for good food and drink lends an air of joviality to his gatherings and feasts.

- Encouraging arts and culture in the city.
